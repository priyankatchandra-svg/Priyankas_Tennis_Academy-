Priyanka's Tennis Academy

Established June 2020 — born out of a desire to build community and promote well-being through tennis during challenging times.

🏛️ Established

In June 2020 amidst the peak of the Covid-19 pandemic, Priyanka's Tennis Academy emerged as a sanctuary for individuals seeking outdoor activities during an era of social distancing. Graduating within the pandemic's early months — from the first collegiate class of Corona — Coach Priyanka had a strong desire to prioritize mental health and well-being.

🎯 Mission

Coach Priyanka's mission is to provide personalized coaching tailored to individual needs, fostering a love for the sport and promoting mental well-being through physical activity. Through strategic partnerships as Lead Tennis Coach at the prestigious Saratoga Country Club, Priyanka's Tennis Academy offers exclusive access to top-notch facilities.

💡 Philosophy

With a coaching philosophy grounded in discovery, empathy, and a relentless pursuit of iteration, Coach Priyanka is dedicated to helping students achieve their tennis goals. With a focus on human-centered design principles, Coach Priyanka inspires creativity and fosters a deep understanding of the game.
